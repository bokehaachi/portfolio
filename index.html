About Me

We can make it together

As I held the tiny ladybug that flew into my finger, I slipped into a dream while the sun was gleaming right into my eyes. An enchanted world of pixels and gradients where I could make anything happen. rooted in reality, the freedom It gave me to contemplate out of the box, bridging what is real and what's not, makes up my art.

I, Jagath an aspiring Graphic Designer, changing the aperture and hues, attempt visualizing what I see and what I'm yet to see.
